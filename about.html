About


Bitcoin Canada is a leading source of bitcoin, blockchain and fintech events, education and news in Canada. We have hosted over 25 educational and community building events in cities across Canada that have introduced thousands of people to bitcoin. We spotlight startups and leaders of the community in Canada to provide a global voice to local initiatives and we compile resources and tutorials to help people understand this exciting technology.




Events

We host events to grow the bitcoin, blockchain and fintech community across Canada. If you would like us to be a media partner for your event, please reach out.



Education

We have done courses and presented at leading Canadian universities and businesses. If you have a question about bitcoin, blockchain and fintech, we can help.



News

We provide trending fintech news and industry spotlights that Canadians want to read. If you have an idea for a story or want us to publish your aricle, please get in touch.
